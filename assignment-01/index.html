Kiki's Top 5 Favorite Live Concerts

  1. Jonas Brothers

    Shoreline Amphitheater, Mountain View, CA 2023

    Went with my husband and appreciated the soundtrack to my childhood intertwining with my current life!

  2. Poppy

    The Echo Lounge & Music Hall, Dallas, TX 2022

    Met the singer and the guitarist, who also played for Lady Gaga, tossed me his pick! Headbanged at the barricade.

  3. The Brokes

    Bowery Ballroom, New York, NY 2025

    Normies see The Strokes. Real Strokes fans see The Brokes. A room full of pure appreciation for the music.

  4. Harry Styles

    The Forum, Los Angeles, CA 2022

    He commands a stage so well! Incredibly talented. The live brass ensemble and light design added so much energy!

  5. House of Protection

    The Fillmore, Silver Spring, MD 2025

    Opening band for Poppy in her nu metal era. They opened up a circle pit and the singer tossed me a water bottle. I got a designed and signed drumhead!
