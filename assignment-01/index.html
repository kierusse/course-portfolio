Kiki's Top 5 Favorite Live Concerts

  1. Jonas Brothers

    Shoreline Amphitheater, Mountain View, CA 2023

    Went with my husband and appreciated the soundtrack to my childhood intertwining with my current life!

  2. Poppy

    The Fillmore, Silver Spring, MD 2025

    I got to introduce some friends to her music and they joined the circle pit! I had met the singer at a past tour where the guitarist, who also played for Lady Gaga, tossed me his pick, and I knew this experience would be just as awesome. Once again, I headbanged at the barricade. The opening band's singer tossed me a water bottle and I got a designed and signed drumhead!

  3. The Brokes

    Bowery Ballroom, New York, NY 2025

    Normies see The Strokes. Real Strokes fans see The Brokes. A room full of pure appreciation for the music.

  4. Harry Styles

    The Forum, Los Angeles, CA 2022

    He commands a stage so well! Incredibly talented. The live brass ensemble and light design added so much energy!

  5. Young the Giant

    Let's Go Music Festival, Crownsvlle, MD 2025

    I wanted to see this band for 10 years, but always missed the opportunity. This festival was in my backyard and I was close enough to the stage to see the bassist's fingers! 15 Year old me would be happy to know I finally made it.
